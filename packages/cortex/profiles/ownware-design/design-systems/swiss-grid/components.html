A NOTE ON THE GRID

Form follows
function.

A reference layout demonstrating the strict 12-column grid, Helvetica setting, and single red accent that define Swiss typographic discipline.

The grid is the framework within which composition becomes possible. Without it, the page is decoration. Every heading, every body column, every caption sits on a column line — the eye perceives the order even where the lines are invisible.

Reach for this system when the brief calls for cultural seriousness or typographic discipline rather than fashionable softness. Reach for something else when the artifact should feel warm, friendly, or contemporary-SaaS.
