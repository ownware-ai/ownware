# Adam — Memory

This file accumulates durable knowledge this profile learns across runs. It is also the **pipeline ledger** — the record that keeps Adam's picture of every relationship current and stops it from ever double-nudging. Categories to maintain:

- **Touch ledger** — for every contact/deal: each touch (channel, date, who reached out, what was said in one line), who owes the next reply, days since last contact, and the agreed next step. The dedupe source of truth before drafting any follow-up.
- **Follow-up state** — who was nudged and when, who replied / went quiet again / asked for space. Never nudge the same thread twice without a reply in between.
- **Pipeline conventions** — how this operator's Attio is structured: pipelines, stages, required fields, what "owed a reply" means for them, and how long quiet is too long for each stage.
- **Operator's voice** — how the operator actually writes a follow-up: length, tone, openings, sign-offs — learned from real sent messages they approved, never invented.
- **What gets replies** — the follow-up shapes, timings, and channels that earned responses for this operator's pipeline, and the ones that didn't, with the reason.
- **Relationship context** — durable facts about key contacts (role, how they prefer to be reached, history with the operator) that make each follow-up informed.

## Operating defaults (seeded — replace with what you learn)

Starting heuristics, not facts about this operator. The moment real pipeline history contradicts one, the learned value wins and this default is overwritten.

- **"Gone quiet" window:** a reply owed and no activity for ~3–5 days is worth a nudge; escalate attention past a week. Tune per relationship warmth.
- **Follow-up persistence:** most replies land between the 5th and 8th touch — one silence is not a dead deal. Space nudges 3–5 days apart, not daily. Past ~12–15 touches with no reply, move to long-term nurture and re-angle in 3–6 months.
- **Best send timing:** mid-morning (≈9–11am their time), Tue–Thu, tends to land better than Monday or late Friday.
- **"Stale" deal:** no activity ≥14 days. **"Stalled":** ≥30 days in the same stage (prefer per-stage average time once learned).
- **Call capture:** every call ends with a concrete next step (what · owner · by when); a call logged without one is flagged, not left blank.
